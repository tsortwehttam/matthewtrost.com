トロスト マシュー レイモンド

Matthew Trost is a startup founder, creative, inventor, and software engineer based in Kamakura, Japan and Brooklyn, New York. With over 15+ years experience in startups ranging from creative technology to CPG, he has built products, led teams, and helped companies scale. He co-founded Haiku and entered YC in the W18 batch. His work has reached #1 on Product Hunt and Hacker News. He later joined Plato Design and Aesthetic (also YC companies), and co-founded Superoot. He likes tennis, epic novels, interactive fiction, audiobooks, and can speak Japanese at a CEFR B2 level.
